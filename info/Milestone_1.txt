## Preparation for Class 7: Meta 1 Milestone

Class 7 is a project work session. You will present your Meta 1 proposal to the class. To prepare, complete the following before the next session.

Define your project research question in a single sentence. It must be specific and answerable with data.

Find your dataset and confirm you can load it in Python using pandas.

Build at least three exploratory charts to understand what your data contains.

Prepare a written summary of one page covering: your research question, a description of your dataset (source, dimensions, key variables), three planned visualisation types with justification, and your chosen colour palette with reasoning from the principles studied in Class 4.

Your Meta 1 presentation should itself follow the three-act structure: Act 1 establishes the problem your research addresses, Act 2 presents what the data suggests, and Act 3 outlines your analytical approach and planned visualisations.
